My Favourite Meal

First, I need to tell you about my favourite meal. As a young man from Africa, there sure are a lot of choice in terms of food. I grew up eating a wide variety of foods that my mom would cook. They all tasted delicious.

However, of all the food I ate while growing up, my favourite was Bread and Beans. I remember vividly the delight that I always experienced anytime I was served this.

Anyway, I thought to share with you how to make my favourite meal just so you will experience the delight I always enjoyed, and so you know how to satisfy me when I visit you. Without further ado, let's get it on.

So here are the things you would need to prepare the beans:

Ingredients

  • 4 cups Honey beans/ Nigerian brown beans/ Black eyed peas
  • ¬º cup palm oil
  • 4 Roma tomatoes
  • 4 scotch bonnet peppers
  • 1 Large red onion
  • 1 Large bell pepper
  • 5 tbsp Ground dried shrimp (aka cray fish)optional
  • 3 tsp Shrimp bullion can be substituted with vegetable bullion if vegan
  • Salt to taste

Cooking Instructions

  1. Rinse the beans and place in a deep pot with 7 cups of water. ‚úîÔ∏è
  2. Boil the beans on medium heat until it becomes soft, it could take about 40 mins. If you are using black eyed peas, it might take a little longer. ‚úîÔ∏è
  3. While the beans are boiling, blend the tomatoes, peppers, and onions and set aside. ‚úîÔ∏è
  4. Once the beans are softened from boiling (do not drain the water), turn the heat down to low, the pour in the blended tomatoes into pot. ‚úîÔ∏è
  5. Allow the beans to cook for an additional 10 minutes with the tomatoes, then add in the crayfish, bullion and palm oil. Stir, and cook for another 10 minutes. ‚úîÔ∏è
  6. Season the beans with salt to your taste. Turn off the heat, and allow to sit for 5 minutes before serving. ‚úîÔ∏è
